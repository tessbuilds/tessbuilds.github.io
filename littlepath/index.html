What makes it different

Not another generic parenting app.

Most parenting apps give you articles. LittlePath gives you what to say and do in the next 60 seconds.

Personalization
Scripts ranked for your child
A spirited 3-year-old who just got back from school needs a different response than a sensitive 5-year-old on a home day. LittlePath knows the difference and surfaces the right script first.
Context awareness
Fatigue window detection
The after-school hour is the hardest. The app detects when your child is in a high-fatigue window and automatically adjusts which scripts appear — calming, low-energy-required approaches first.
Feedback loop
It gets better over time
Rate each script after using it. The app tracks what works and what doesn't for your specific child, improving its rankings over weeks of use.
Philosophy
Montessori grounding
Every script and activity is rooted in Montessori principles — not generic positive parenting platitudes. The rationale is always visible so you understand the why, not just the what.

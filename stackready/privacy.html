PrepAI — Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: March 2026
PrepAI does not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with advertisers or third parties.

1. Information We Collect

PrepAI collects minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Practice sessions — your questions, answers, scores, and session history are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Voice input — when you use voice answers, audio is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition framework on-device. We do not store audio recordings.
  • Subscription status — managed by Apple's StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide PrepAI's features:

  • Generating interview questions via the Claude AI API (Anthropic)
  • Grading your answers and providing feedback
  • Displaying your progress history and score trends

Your answers are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data. Visit anthropic.com/privacy for details.

3. Data Storage

All session history, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude API — processes your interview questions and answers to generate AI responses. Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — handles subscription purchases and billing. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Speech Recognition — transcribes voice answers on-device. Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

PrepAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
